Research

My research interests are in cryptography, and specifically signatures and multi-party computation.
Recently I've been interested in policy (see "Cryptographers’ Feedback on the EU Digital Identity’s ARF")--I hope to have more updates in the future!
For information about the "DKLs" threshold ECDSA signing schemes, we have a website! We always love hearing from people who have deployed or used our schemes... Please feel free to reach out if you know of any implementations we've missed! :)
