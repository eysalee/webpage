I am a PhD candidate at Northeastern University advised by abhi shelat.
My research interests are in cryptography, and specifically multi-party computation.
For Summer 2018, I visited Bar-Ilan University for a summer program on applied MPC and implementation.
For Summer 2019, I worked with Peter Rindal as an intern at Visa Research in Palo Alto.
For Summer 2022, I interned with an applied research team (FLARE) at JP Morgan in New York City.

In my spare time, I enjoy doodling, as evident by the Alice and Bob characters in my presentations.
